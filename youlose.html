YOU HAVE DIED.

You are not the only fish to have died as a direct result of human water pollution. In fact, 100,000 marine creatures a year die from plastic entanglement - and that's just the ones we know about.

So what can you do to help?

Reduce your use of single-use plastics

Remember to recycle

Participate in local beach/river cleanups

... and most importantly, spread the word! We can't do this without all of you.
